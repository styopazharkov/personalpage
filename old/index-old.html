The Group Testing Problem

I made this YouTube video as a project for my CS250 class. Essentially, it explains the Kautz-Singleton construction for group testing matrices on a level that's understandable to all (no worries if you don't know what that means—the whole point is that you don't have to). If you know about polynomials, logarithms, and a little bit of modular arithmetic, you should check it out.
